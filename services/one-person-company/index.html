Why One Person Company (OPC) Registration Is Critical

One Person Company (OPC) is a Pvt Ltd company with single owner/director. Gives limited liability protection to solo entrepreneurs without need for multiple directors. Best for solo founders wanting company structure before bringing partners.

  • ‚úì**Solo Ownership**: 100% ownership with one director only
  • ‚úì**Limited Liability**: Personal assets protected from business debts
  • ‚úì**Separate Legal Entity**: OPC is separate person in eyes of law
  • ‚úì**Easy Conversion**: Can convert to Pvt Ltd when ready to bring partners
  • ‚úì**Professional Image**: Company structure gives credibility over proprietorship

Frequently Asked Questions

What is nominee in OPC?

Person who will manage OPC if director dies/incapacitated. Mandatory requirement. Nominee can be family member or friend.

Can I have multiple directors in OPC?

No, only 1 director allowed. If you want multiple directors, must convert to Pvt Ltd.

When can OPC be converted to Pvt Ltd?

Anytime. Mandatory conversion if turnover > ‚Çπ2Cr or paid-up capital > ‚Çπ50L. Voluntary conversion allowed anytime to bring partners.

Can foreigner be director of OPC?

No, only Indian resident can be OPC director.

What are OPC compliance requirements?

Annual ROC filing, Income Tax Return, audit if turnover > ‚Çπ2Cr. Similar to Pvt Ltd but with one director.

Can OPC raise funding from VCs?

Not recommended. VCs prefer Pvt Ltd with multiple founders. OPC is for solo bootstrapped businesses.

What happens if I want to add partner later?

Convert OPC to Pvt Ltd. Simple process on MCA portal. Then add second director and issue shares to partner.
